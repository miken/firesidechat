NFTE Student Pitch & Donation

EVMA is proud to have Network For Teaching Entrepreneurship (NFTE) with us for this event. NFTE inspires students from low-income communities to find their paths to success through business opportunities and entrepreneurship. Part of the proceeds from ticket sales and raffle will be going to USC Marshall's Challenge for Charity efforts. A representative from NFTE will recieve the check and speak about NFTE's goals and ways to get involved.
